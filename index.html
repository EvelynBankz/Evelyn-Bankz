About Me

Evelyn Bankole

Hi, I'm Evelyn Bankole, a passionate Data Scientist with a strong foundation in Data Analytics and a knack for transforming complex data into actionable insights. Over the years, I've honed my skills in data analysis, visualization, and database management, working on impactful projects that drive business growth and improve decision-making processes.

Starting as a Data Analyst at Data Pluga, I played a key role in optimizing the ETL process for a Real-Time Visibility Decision Support System. This project wasn't just about numbers—it was about making data more accessible and useful, which in turn helped the business make better decisions faster. I also led the development of a Business Intelligence Sales Management app, a tool that accurately predicted key financial outcomes, solidifying my belief in the power of data to shape business strategies.

Beyond data crunching, my experience as a Customer Success Manager at LATOKEN allowed me to develop strong problem-solving skills, managing customer inquiries and ensuring a smooth user experience. This role taught me the importance of clear communication and how crucial it is to have a customer-centric approach, especially in a tech-driven environment.

My journey has also taken me through roles in digital marketing, content creation, and even estate surveying. This diverse background has equipped me with a unique blend of technical, creative, and managerial skills. Whether it's developing a marketing campaign, analyzing sales data, or managing a team, I'm always eager to take on new challenges and deliver results.

I’m constantly looking for ways to expand my knowledge, particularly in coding and machine learning, to stay at the forefront of the industry. My goal is to continue growing as a data professional, leveraging my skills to help businesses unlock the full potential of their data.
